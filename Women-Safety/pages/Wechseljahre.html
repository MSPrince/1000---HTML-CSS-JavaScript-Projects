Menopause

During menopause, there is a gradual change in the female organism; the production of the female hormones in the ovaries become depleted, menstrual periods become irregular and remain eventually, it is completely over. Unpleasant consequences of this can include night sweats, hot flashes, nervousness, Sleep disturbances, hair loss, skin changes or loss of libido
Sometimes, in addition to an accurate medical history and a gynecological examination, the analysis of the Hormonal status from the blood may be necessary to make an individual diagnosis. We would be happy to discuss the findings with you and advise you on the advantages and disadvantages of hormone replacement therapy in order to work together to find the best possible therapy for you.
