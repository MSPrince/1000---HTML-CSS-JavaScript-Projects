The hormonal IUD is a regional contraceptive that enters the uterine cavity is pickled. There, in minimal doses, it releases the progestogen levonorgestrel. The spiral is available in 3 variants: the "Jaydess" for 3 years, the "Kyleena" and the "Mirena" for 5 years each. In the case of the Jaydess, a light menstrual period. The Kyleena has is identical in size to the small Jaydess, has but, like the Mirena, a hormone reservoir for 5 years. Menstrual bleeding comes in comparison to the 3-year hormonal IUD a little less often. In the case of the Mirena, the menstrual period. This can be of great benefit if you are suffering from painful menstruation or suffer from alternating heavy bleeding. The hormonal IUD is one of the safest contraceptive methods and has very few side effects, as the hormones act mainly in the uterus and only one very small part of the concentration of hormones enters the body. THE IMPLANON

THE IMPLANON® (CONTRACEPTIVE STICKS)

The Implanon is a soft plastic rod that contains the corpus luteum hormone etonogestrel comprises. It is inserted directly at the gynecologist under the skin on the inside of the upper arm. There it then sets continuously releases hormones and guarantees contraceptive protection for up to 3 years. Although it is characterized by its altered hormone composition is usually better tolerated than the contraceptive pill, it can nevertheless, irregularities or a lack of menstruation occur.

HORMONE-FREE

Not every woman can tolerate hormonal contraception. In some women, due to Hereditary Coagulation disorders, the administration of hormones is even medically contraindicated. Common adverse effects of hormonal contraception include weight gain, Water retention or an impairment of the state of mind. Many women who have been using hormones for years often only notice after stopping the pill how much it affects them goods. For these women, a hormone-free alternative is a huge gain in quality of life. They have their natural cycle and are not affected by the side effects of hormonal contraception.

THE COPPER SPIRAL

A very effective method of contraception and the longest-established intrauterine device. Spirals are now available in many sizes and shapes and is therefore very well tolerated. The Copper IUD provides safe contraceptive protection for 3 years.
